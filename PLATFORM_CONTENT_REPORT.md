## 9. Key Observations

1. **MIR has the most content** — 4,668 flashcards and 472 questions. ENARM and ENURM have questions but no flashcards yet.
2. **Flashcards are only linked to MIR** in PostgreSQL. The 4,699 Firebase flashcards were migrated but assigned to MIR. ENARM, ENURM, and USMLE have 0 flashcards.
3. **ENARM has very limited content** — only 22 questions across 10 specialties, no flashcards, no videos, no courses.
4. **Video content exists in the database** (36 modules, 47 lessons) but is not yet linked to any specific exam.
5. **User base is healthy** — 960 registered users with 1,024 active subscriptions and over 5,000 completed exam attempts.
6. **The legacy Firebase app** has 1,109 questions and 4,699 flashcards that have been successfully migrated to the new PostgreSQL platform.
